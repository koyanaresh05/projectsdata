Step 1:  Buy ELM327 BT OBD Scanner
Step 2:	 Connect the Scanner in OBD port of CAR below the steering wheel 
Step 3:  Turn ON the engine and pair any mobile phone to OBD Scanner via Bluetooth
Step 4:  After pairing note the MAC address of OBD Scanner
Step 5:  Modify the MAC address and in new_check_OBD_MAC3_update2 and WIFI credentials in secrets.h
Step 6:  Now upload the code in ESP32 board and turn ON the CAR engine to connect
